# Strategies

Map of content for every trading strategy and playbook in the vault, grouped by strategy type. This is the hub that anchors the strategies cluster in the graph: it links to every note under `04-Strategies/`, so the notes pull together visually, and the path color group `path:04-Strategies/` tints them as one family.

A strategy note is an executable playbook: an actual trade with legs, entry logic, and payoff. The underlying theory lives as a concept in `01-Concepts/`. Keep that line: the trade goes here, the idea it rests on goes there, and they link to each other.
